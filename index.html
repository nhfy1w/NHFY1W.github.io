Introduction

LDP FLY EFB For HarmonyOS NEXT is an Electronic Flight Bag (EFB) application designed to enhance flight operation efficiency. We are committed to protecting your privacy and data security. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your information.

Operating Environment and Device Requirements

This application only runs on devices equipped with HarmonyOS NEXT / HarmonyOS 5. We recommend using this app on compatible operating system versions for the best experience.

Data Collection and Usage

  • Personal Information: We do not collect or store any personal identity information.
  • Device Information: To provide better service, we may collect non-personal information such as device model and operating system version.
  • Usage Data: We do not record specific operational behaviors within the application.

Third-party Services

Our application does not include any third-party advertising or tracking services, ensuring a clean and uninterrupted user experience.

Security Measures

Although we do not collect sensitive information, we still take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect known information from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure, or destruction.

User Rights

You can uninstall the application at any time to remove our services. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact us via the following email: 1510006135@qq.com.

Contact Us
